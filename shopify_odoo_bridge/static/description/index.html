Shopify Odoo Bridge

Shopify Odoo Bridge allows you to connect your Odoo with Shopify. Allows to import categories, products, orders, customers, shipping methods, payment methods. Allows to export/update products from Odoo to Shopify. Allows to manage multiple instances with different set of configurations. Maintains the record of each import and export.

All data at a glance

The Dashboard provides a single space for viewing your all Shopify instance's data at a glance, thus making it easy for you to expeditiously track your sales and data imports!

Say No To Manual Entry

No need to enter data manually in Odoo. Simply Import/Update data like Sale orders, Products, Categories, Customers/Partners, and shipping methods from Shopify to Odoo through our bridge.

Order Management

Orders made at Website/Marketplace can be easily imported to Odoo. User is facilitated with an option to Import Sales orders coming from Shopify and other different channels connected to Odoo and thus allows him to keep track of orders placed at multiple Platforms/Marketplaces by shipping sales orders is also easy as it was never before.

Inventory Management

User can instantly sync the product whenever any order is synced to Odoo and can synchronize the inventory quantities across sales channel in near real time. Updation of product quantity at Odoo end will get reflected in Real-time on the desired E-Commerce Platform/ Marketplace.

Category Management

All the products at your Shopify are just one click away to get imported to Odoo and thus can be managed efficiently. Categorize your Products for better search and thus to save time by Importing the categories to Odoo on just single Click!

Customer Management

Keep track and information of all your customers/Partners by Importing them to Odoo and If any information is updated of any customer/vendor then all you need to do is update them in order to reflect the update on the other end.

Shipping Management

Use our connector to Ship orders from all channels easily and to effectively Avoid Shipping delays. Odoo multi-channel connector provides fully integrated shipping functionality which allows the user to sync all the shipping methods from multiple channels connected to Odoo with our connector.
